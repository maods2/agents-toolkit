# Playbook: Architecture Review

## Use when

A system, feature area, or proposed design needs structural evaluation.

## References

- Principles: `principles/software-engineering.md`, `principles/architecture.md`, `principles/testing.md`
- Skills: `skills/clean-architecture/`, `skills/solid/`, `skills/workflow-discovery/`

## Workflow

1. Define the review scope and decision to support.
2. Discover the relevant workflow or component boundary.
3. Map responsibilities, dependencies, data flow, and contracts.
4. Evaluate risks against architecture principles.
5. Identify simple improvements and trade-offs.
6. Record findings, assumptions, and open questions.
